How to calculate future value ?

Calculating FV of the asset.

You can estimate the future value of an asset by using the future value formula that is FV = PV * (1 + i)n
Where,
'PV' denotes the present value of an asset.
'i' denotes the interest rate that applies to a particular asset.
'n' represents the number of periods or also called as compounding periods. If,
PV = 100;
i = 2;
n = 2 years;
Then,
FV = 100(1+2/100)2
That is $104.03
Future Value of the asset is $104.03.You now have the future value of the asset.

Future Value of money calculator

Determining monetary worth of a possession.

Let's say your wrist watch costs $1500 currently and you want to know how much would it cost at a certain point in time in future say 2 years later and the interest rate of your watch is 5%. Then $1500 * (1 + 5/100)2 we get $1653.75. and you are left with the required number that indicates the future value of your asset, future value of money calculator will determine the assets worth in the future when the current value of that specific asset is provided along with rate of return and time period associated with it.Also you can estimate the future worth of an asset when you pay a certain amount on a periodic basis using future value with pmt formula.

Future Value with PMT

Future Value formula with PMT

FV = PMT [(1+(i/100))n -1/(i/100)] + PV(1+(i/100))n

Future Value along with Periodic Monthly Payments (PMT) and Present Value (PV) will help you find out the future worth of an asset when amount is paid on a periodic basis or when the amount is paid depending on compounding periods and when the current value (PV) of that particular asset is known.
