Future Value Formula

FV=PV(1+i)n


Above formula will estimate future value of an asset when the present value(PV), rate of interest(i) and number of periods(n) are provided.Future value helps estimate the worth of an asset in later point in time.

time value of money

Would you rather eat fresh food today or let it rot for some time and then consider eating ?


Time Value of Money not exactly but kind of works in a similar way say for instance, you have fresh apple you or any sane person would probably munch on it before it gets putrid and lose all it's nutritional value and novelty.Here the apple you have is the 'ASSET' and the freshness or newness of apple is the 'VALUE'.So if you eat your fruit now when it's fresh you could possibly reap the benefits more rather than consuming it later point in time. This example is what tells you that an assets current value is more than it would be in future.Monetary value of an asset is worth more now that it would be in later point in time.one major factor that affects the economic value of an asset is 'TIME'.$10 today could be $15 in one or five years.
